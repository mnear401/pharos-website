The Anchor Sentinel

The Anchor Sentinel is a document integrity tool that runs entirely on your own computer. It generates a unique cryptographic fingerprint of any file — a contract, a deed, a photograph, a report — and records it in a tamper-evident ledger that lives on your hard drive. No cloud. No third party. No monthly fee to a company you have to trust. Just math, running locally, producing a Certificate of Verity that proves your document existed, unchanged, at a specific moment in time.

"Every other digital notary asks you to trust their company or a public blockchain. Pharos is the only one that asks you to trust the math on the machine sitting right in front of you. If my company disappears tomorrow, your proof of truth remains immutable on your hard drive."
