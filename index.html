The Anchor Sentinel

The Anchor Sentinel runs entirely on your own computer. It generates a cryptographic fingerprint of any file — a contract, a deed, a photograph — and records it in a tamper-evident ledger that lives on your hard drive. No cloud. No third party. Just math.

"Every other digital notary asks you to trust their company or a public blockchain. Pharos is the only one that asks you to trust the math on the machine sitting right in front of you. If my company disappears tomorrow, your proof of truth remains immutable on your hard drive."

Each anchored document receives a Certificate of Verity — proof that it existed, unchanged, at a specific moment in time.
